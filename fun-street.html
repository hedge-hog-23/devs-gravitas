Fun-street


Fun-Street is an exciting and lively event that is sure to bring smiles to everyone's faces. It is a part of the Gravitas Cultural Event, an annual celebration held at our college that showcases the creative talents of students from various departments. During Fun-Street, the campus is transformed into a vibrant and festive atmosphere with colorful decorations, music, and games. There are stalls selling delicious snacks and drinks, as well as various carnival-style games and activities for people of all ages to enjoy. Participants can test their skills in games such as balloon darts, ring toss, and basketball. They can also try their luck at winning prizes at games of chance like spin-the-wheel or lucky draw. For those looking for a more interactive experience, there are photo booths, face painting stations, and caricature artists to capture memorable moments. In addition to the games and activities, there are also performances by talented student groups, such as dance troupes, music bands, and stand-up comedians. These performances add to the festive atmosphere and provide a platform for students to showcase their creativity and talent. Fun-Street is an event that promotes community spirit and brings people together for a day of fun and entertainment. It is a great opportunity for students to take a break from their academic routines and enjoy themselves with friends and colleagues.
